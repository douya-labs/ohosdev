The fastest, most reliable way to reach us is **GitHub**:

- **Bug reports / content errors / feature ideas** → [open an issue](https://github.com/douya-labs/harmony-app-dev/issues)
- **Tutorial drafts, translations, new references** → [open a pull request](https://github.com/douya-labs/harmony-app-dev/pulls)
- **General discussion** → use the [Discussions](https://github.com/douya-labs/harmony-app-dev/discussions) tab

For things that genuinely don't fit on GitHub (security disclosures, partnership inquiries, takedown requests), email us at:

> **hello [at] ohosdev.com**

We're a small group; please give us a few business days to reply.
