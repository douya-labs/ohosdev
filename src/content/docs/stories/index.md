# Insights

Two kinds of writing live here:

- **Dev diaries** — first-person notes from shipping [floracarta](https://github.com/douya-labs/floracarta), a HarmonyOS app built over 30 days. Things that broke, things shipped, things shipped and then unshipped.
- **Ecosystem observations** — neutral, fact-only takes on HarmonyOS news, tooling, and the wider mobile-dev landscape. We cite sources, label opinions as opinions, and let readers draw their own conclusions.
