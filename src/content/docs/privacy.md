_Last updated: 2025-04-20_

This privacy policy describes how **ohosdev.com** (the "site") handles personal information. We try to keep it short and honest.

## What we collect

- **Aggregated traffic metrics.** We use a privacy-respecting analytics tool to count page views, referrers, and high-level country/region. We do not collect IP addresses or browser fingerprints in a way that identifies individual visitors.
- **Server logs.** Our hosting provider (Cloudflare Pages) keeps short-term access logs for security and abuse prevention. These contain IP addresses and user-agent strings and are typically retained for a few weeks before being deleted.
- **Comments / contact form.** We do not currently run a comment system or contact form. If we add one, this policy will be updated and the data flow described here.

We do **not** sell or share personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

## Cookies and similar technologies

The site itself sets no first-party tracking cookies. Embedded third-party services may set cookies on their own initiative. The most relevant ones today:

- **Google AdSense** (when enabled). AdSense may set cookies for ad personalization and frequency capping. You can manage your ad preferences at [Google's Ads Settings](https://adssettings.google.com/) and opt out of personalized advertising at [aboutads.info](https://www.aboutads.info/choices/).
- **Google Analytics** (if enabled in the future). Used in IP-anonymized mode if used at all.

## Advertising

This site may display advertisements served by Google AdSense. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting [Google Ads Settings](https://adssettings.google.com/) or [aboutads.info](https://www.aboutads.info/choices/).

## Children

The site is intended for adult software developers. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## Your rights

If you are in the EU, UK, or another jurisdiction with data-subject rights (GDPR, UK GDPR, CCPA, etc.), you may have the right to access, correct, or delete personal data we hold about you. Because we do not maintain personal user accounts, in most cases we hold no individually identifiable data about you. Contact us via the [Contact page](/contact/) if you have a specific request.

## Changes to this policy

If we change this policy, we'll update the "last updated" date at the top of this page. Material changes will be announced on the homepage for a reasonable period.

## Contact

Questions: see the [Contact page](/contact/).
